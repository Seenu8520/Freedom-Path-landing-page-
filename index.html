Common Questions Answered

We've got answers to your most pressing concerns

Is online ITR filing safe?

▼
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) to protect all your data. Your information is never shared with anyone. We're GST registered and fully compliant with all tax laws.

How long does ITR filing take?

▼
Most filings are completed within 24-48 hours. We process on same-day basis for urgent filings. You'll receive your acknowledgement within 2 hours of submission.

Are there any hidden charges?

▼
No hidden charges, ever! Our pricing is completely transparent. All costs are discussed upfront. What you see is what you pay - no surprise fees.

What documents do I need?

▼
For salaried employees: Form 16, Aadhaar & PAN. For business owners: Business income details, bank statements & investment proofs. We'll guide you through the exact documents needed based on your profile.

Can you help me get maximum refund?

▼
Yes! Our CAs specialize in identifying all eligible deductions & investments. We help you claim every rupee you're entitled to, maximizing your refund legitimately.

What if I miss the filing deadline?

▼
Even after the deadline, you can still file (belated filing). You'll pay a small penalty but can still claim refunds. We can guide you through the process with minimal impact.
